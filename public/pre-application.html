Pre-Application

What to Expect

Welcome to JobScooter! Our AI-powered application process is designed to create a comprehensive professional profile that showcases your skills and qualifications. Here's what you can expect:

Step 1: ID Verification (0% - 20%)

  • Upload your government-issued ID document
  • Our AI will extract personal information automatically
  • Manual entry option if AI extraction fails
  • Account creation with email verification
  • Time required: 5-10 minutes

Step 2: Language Verification (20% - 40%)

  • Select your language proficiency (English, German, or both)
  • Upload German language certificates if applicable
  • Certificate verification against accredited institutions
  • Time required: 5-15 minutes

Step 3: Certificate Analysis (40% - 60%)

  • Upload all your certificates and qualifications
  • AI-powered document classification and verification
  • Authenticity checks against institution databases
  • Skills and qualifications assessment
  • Time required: 10-20 minutes

Step 4: Media & Introduction (60% - 80%)

  • Upload a professional profile picture
  • Record a 2-3 minute video introduction
  • AI-powered video transcription and subtitles
  • Time required: 15-25 minutes

Step 5: Profile Finalization (80% - 100%)

  • Auto-generated professional CV creation
  • Complete profile compilation and review
  • Privacy settings configuration
  • Quality assessment with traffic light system
  • Time required: 5-10 minutes

Total Time Estimate

40-80 minutes (can be completed in multiple sessions)
